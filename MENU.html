About Kauswagan

Discover the rich history and vibrant culture of our beloved municipality.

Our Beautiful Municipality

Kauswagan is a coastal municipality in the province of Lanao del Norte, Philippines. The name "Kauswagan" means "progress" in the local language, reflecting the community's commitment to development and growth.

With its strategic location along Panguil Bay, Kauswagan has been a significant trading hub and is known for its natural beauty, agricultural productivity, and warm, hospitable people.

Today, Kauswagan is recognized for its innovative development programs and sustainable practices, making it a model municipality in the region.
